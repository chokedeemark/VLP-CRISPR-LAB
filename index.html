Core Research Areas

Advancing biotechnology through precise genome engineering and novel delivery mechanisms.

VLP Cas9 DNA Editing

Development of engineered Virus-Like Particles for highly specific and efficient delivery of Cas9 ribonucleoproteins for precise DNA modifications.

Learn more

VLP Cas13 RNA Editing

Exploiting Cas13 systems packaged in VLPs for transient, potent, and safe transcriptomic regulation without permanent DNA alterations.

Learn more

Designing next-generation immunization strategies targeting Dengue leveraging our proprietary VLP architecture for robust immune responses.
